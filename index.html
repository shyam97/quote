Day 14/41

It is far better to perform one's duty with faults rather than to perform another's duty perfectly. It is far better to succumb while performing your duty than to involve in others' works, for following a path other than yours is bereft of knowledge.

Source

स्वधर्मे निधनं श्रेयिः परधर्मो भयावहः ॥

Bhagavad Gita 3.35
